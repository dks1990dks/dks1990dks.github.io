About Us

Company Profile

CORPORATE PRESENCE - OUR JOURNEY

With a combined experience of 30 years among our team members, we have firmly established ourselves as a recognized player in the textile industry since 1991. Our expertise in poly fabrics and dress materials underscores our journey.

Our Production is completely compliant with international standards, and we use only high-quality raw materials. We continuously re-engineer our processes to match the global demand.

Our Product line consists of a wide range of high-fashion polyester-based fabrics used in all types of women's apparel, kids' wear, beachwear, uniforms, scarves, and stoles.




PRODUCTION LINE - WEAVING UNIT

Our Weaving Unit brings fabrics to life using advanced machinery, including warping, twisting, texturing, winding, and sizing machines. Supported by connections to adept weavers and mill owners, our dynamic supply chain forms the foundation of our fabric sourcing.

Anchored by 120 Automatic PowerLooms and WaterJets Machines, we craft an impressive 500,000 meters of fabric per month. Our offerings extend to luxurious choices like Poly Georgette, Poly Chiffon, and Poly Crepe.

Our operation integrates 40 Circular Knitting Machines, contributing to a monthly output of 100 tons. Our range includes single jersey fabric, Poly Dull Knitting, Poly Bright Lycra, and Poly Knit Spandex.

Our Finished Fabric Width spans from 44 Inches to 60 Inches.

Additionally, Finished Fabric Weight ranges from 35 GSM to 200 GSM.




PRODUCTION LINE - PROCESSING LINE UNIT

Our Dyeing Unit features an array of Hi-Tech U-Jets, Soft Flow, Long Tube & Jigar Machines, offering a dyeing capacity of 2 million meters per month.

Additionally, our Printing Unitshowcases Double Servo Rotary Printing & Flat Bed machines, achieving a printing capacity of 1 million meters per month.

Upto 16 colours can be printed on the printing machines.

Screen Repeats of 64 cm, 81 cm, 92 cm can be printed.

Our Digital Printing Unit comprises 8 Sublimation Digital Printing & 2 Direct Digital Printing machines, producing 3 Lakhs meters per month.
